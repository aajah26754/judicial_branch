State Court System

  • State Supreme Court
  • The highest court in a state
  • Reviews appeal from lower state courts
  • Decisions are final unless a federal issue is involved
  • Intermediate Appellate Courts
  • Recieves appeals from trial courts
  • Not all states have these courts
  • Trial Courts
  • Handles most cases, including criminal, civil, family, and probate matters
  • Divided into different courts (famiy, small claims, etc.)

Federal Court System

  • US Supreme Court
  • The highest court in the US
  • Has the final say on constitutional and federal law issues
  • Reviews cases from federal appellate and state supreme courts
  • US Courts of Appeals (Circuit Courts)
  • 13 circuits: 12 regional circuits + 1 Federal Circuit
  • Has the final say on constitutional and federal law issues
  • US Supreme Court
  • The highest court in the US
  • Has the final say on constitutional and federal law issues
